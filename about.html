About Me

Hi! I'm Esther, an aspiring frontend developer and designer from Hong Kong, currently doing a Master's degree in Interaction Design in Estonia.

Before pivoting my career to the tech field, I worked in NGOs and government organization as project coordinator and social media manager.

From event planning to web design, my philosophy is the same - be human-centred. I believe that good design not only can improve people's lives, but also bringing users' a particular emotion, idea, or memory in their mind.

When I'm not coding, I'm on the way to cinema / theatre / library / gym room / travel!
